## 🏢 Overview

Canopy Corp is a boutique systems engineering and R&D firm specializing in next-generation computing architectures. We build highly optimized, secure, and autonomous frameworks that bridge the gap between heavy software processing and bare-metal hardware acceleration. Our overarching mission is to provide robust, reliable, and scalable environments for complex analytical systems.

## 🔬 Core R&D Divisions

*   **Sub-Aquatic Anomaly Detection:** Leveraging advanced computer vision and generative adversarial networks (GANs) for complex underwater environmental monitoring, spatial mapping, and synthetic data generation.
*   **Zero-Trust Operating Environments:** Architecting behavioral-security, AI-native Linux blueprints utilizing eBPF and C++ to ensure absolute system integrity at the kernel level.
*   **Neuromorphic Hardware Acceleration:** Prototyping and deploying event-driven temporal networks (SNNs) on system-on-chip (SoC) field-programmable gate arrays to drastically reduce computational latency.

## ⚙️ Technical Infrastructure

*   **Systems & Kernel:** C++, eBPF, Hardened Debian Linux, Wayland
*   **Intelligence & Vision:** Python, YOLO Architectures, XGBoost
*   **Hardware Engineering:** Verilog, Xilinx PYNQ-Z2 SoC, Discrete Logic Prototyping
